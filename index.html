My Journey

A social media story

From a 10-year-old on YouTube to professional content strategist — here's every chapter.

2012
YouTube · Age 10

It started with a camera.

I was just 10 years old - with a camera, a YouTube account, and absolutely no plan. I made videos about whatever was on my mind. Games, random thoughts, whatever felt fun. I had no idea what I was doing, but I knew I loved to create. Looking back, that channel was the foundation for everything that followed.

>
2020
Zealot News

Indie Game Critic

In 2020 I cofounded Zealot News, an indie game media brand dedicated to spotlighting small developers and the projects they were pouring their hearts into. We built and managed a full website, ran multiple social media channels, and produced YouTube videos that brought these games to a real audience.

What made Zealot special was the access. We coordinated directly with indie developers for interviews, giving unknown creators a platform they wouldn't have had otherwise. It was my first real taste of running a brand end to end, and it taught me a lot about content strategy, team coordination, and what it means to build something from nothing.

Instagram YouTube Twitter
2022
Pixels After Dark

Learning the algorithm.

Pixels After Dark was where everything came together. Armed with years of experience, I identified an emerging niche with low competition and built a content strategy around it from day one.

I stayed locked in on trends, moving fast on viral topics before they peaked and consistently creating content that was built to engage. The results spoke for themselves. The channel grew to over 300,000 followers and surpassed 50 million views, all through organic content and a deep understanding of what audiences actually want to watch.

Pixels After Dark proved that the right niche, the right timing, and the right content strategy can build something real. It remains the project I am most proud of.
