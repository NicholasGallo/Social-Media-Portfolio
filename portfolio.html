My Journey

A social media story

From a 10-year-old on YouTube to professional content strategist — here's every chapter.

2012
YouTube · Age 10

It started with a camera.

My very first YouTube channel — no ring light, no script, no plan. Just a kid who loved making videos. I uploaded gaming walkthroughs and "random stuff" every week and somehow convinced 200 people to subscribe. It was the spark that started everything.

2020
Zealot News

Indie Game Critic

When Instagram hit peak popularity I jumped in head-first. I experimented with photography, presets, and consistent theming long before "personal brand" was a phrase I knew. I grew a small but engaged community around my hobby photography.

2022
Pixels After Dark

Learning the algorithm.

My first real taste of virality. I studied every trend, posting time, and hashtag strategy I could find. A video about local events hit 80K views overnight. Numbers became feedback, and I became addicted to optimizing content for reach without sacrificing authenticity.
